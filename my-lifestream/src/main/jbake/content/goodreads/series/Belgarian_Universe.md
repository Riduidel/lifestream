## Description


    The Belgarian universe is the broad universe containing two major pentologies, as well as a pair of prequels and a related non-fiction work on the writing of the universe.
    
    The primary books are listed below in internal chronological order. The order the series were written was:
    * [The Belgariad](http://www.goodreads.com/series/40739-the-belgariad)
    * [The Mallorean](http://www.goodreads.com/series/42429-the-malloreon)
    * [The Belgarian Prequels](http://www.goodreads.com/series/62115-belgarian-prequels)
    * The Rivan Codex
