## Description


    The books of Robert Silverberg's Majipoor series are broken down into two series.
    
    The first published series was the [Lord Valentine Cycle](http://www.goodreads.com/series/50085-majipoor-lord-valentine).
    
    Later, Silverberg wrote a prequel trilogy, which is the [Lord Prestimion Cycle](http://www.goodreads.com/series/42489-majipoor-lord-prestimion).
    
    Novellas set in the world of Majipoor can be found in [Legends](http://www.goodreads.com/book/show/77232.Legends) and [Legends II](http://www.goodreads.com/book/show/77563.Legends_II). There is also a short story in [Flights: Extreme Visions of Fantasy](http://www.goodreads.com/book/show/597659.Flights).
