# Valdemar (Chronological)

## Books

 * [Soeurs de sang](../books/9782266065900.md)
 * [Oathbreakers (Valdemar: Vows and Honor, #2)](../books/9780886774547.md)

## Description


    This series lists the books in the Valdemar universe according to their internal chronology. It is unnumbered because there are still books being written early in the chronology.
    
    It includes the series, in order:
    * [Mage Wars](http://www.goodreads.com/series/43489-valdemar-mage-wars)
    * [The Last Herald-Mage](http://www.goodreads.com/series/41448-valdemar-last-herald-mage)
    * [Collegium Chronicles](http://www.goodreads.com/series/52236-the-collegium-chronicles)
    * [Vows & Honor](http://www.goodreads.com/series/40368-valdemar-vows-and-honor)
    * [Exile](http://www.goodreads.com/series/56067-valdemar-exile)
    * [Arrows of the Queen](http://www.goodreads.com/series/56070-valdemar-arrows-of-the-queen)
    * [Mage Winds](http://www.goodreads.com/series/41447-valdemar-mage-winds)
    * [Mage Storms](http://www.goodreads.com/series/41319-valdemar-mage-storms)
    * [Darien's Tale](http://www.goodreads.com/series/41443-valdemar-darien-s-tale)
    
    It also includes standalone books within this world (<i>Take a Thief</i>, <i>Brightly Burning</i>, and <i>By the Sword</i>) in their chronological order.
    
    The [Valdemar Short Stories](http://www.goodreads.com/series/56073-valdemar-short-stories) are not placed on this series listing.
    
    See also: [Valdemar (Publication order)](http://www.goodreads.com/series/56074-valdemar-publication-order).
