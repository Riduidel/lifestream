## Description


    The Lord Valentine sequence was the first published in the [Majipoor Series](http://www.goodreads.com/series/50087-the-majipoor-series).
    
    It was set some time after the prequel series of [Lord Prestimion](http://www.goodreads.com/series/42489-majipoor-lord-prestimion).
    
    "The Seventh Shrine", a novella set in the world of Majipoor during the time of Lord Valentine, can be found in [Legends](http://www.goodreads.com/book/show/77232.Legends). This story was later adapted into a [graphic novel](http://www.goodreads.com/book/show/252974.The_Seventh_Shrine).
    
    There is also a short story, "The Sorcerer's Apprentice", in [Flights: Extreme Visions of Fantasy](http://www.goodreads.com/book/show/597659.Flights).
