## Description


    Also known as:
    * [Les Annales de la Compagnie Noire](http://www.goodreads.com/series/94020-les-annales-de-la-compagnie-noire) (see for French numbering)
    
    When sorcerers and demigods go to war, those wars are fought by mercenaries, "dog soldiers," grunts in the trenches. And the stories of those soldiers are the stories of Glen Cook's hugely popular fantasy series, Chronicles of The Black Company.
