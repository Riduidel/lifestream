# WP-cron YADD

Here is my small contribution to the wonderful world of Wordpress : [WP-cron YADD](http://nicolas-delsaux.is-a-geek.net/wordpress/wp-content/plugins/wp-cron-yadd.txt) is an integration of [YADD](http://nozell.com/blog/archives/2004/09/17/yet-another-dailydelicious-hack-for-wordpress/) into [WP-cron](http://www.skippy.net/blog/plugins/) architecture. By this mean, no more hassle writing shell scripts and using system cron table. Instead ... well, instead, nothing to do ! To install, put it in your plugins directory, provided YADD and WP-cron are installed, it will simply work.

Voici ma petite contribution au monde merveilleux de Wordpress : [WP-cron YADD](http://nicolas-delsaux.is-a-geek.net/wordpress/wp-content/plugins/wp-cron-yadd.txt) est une intégration de [YADD](http://nozell.com/blog/archives/2004/09/17/yet-another-dailydelicious-hack-for-wordpress/) dans l'architecture [WP-cron](http://www.skippy.net/blog/plugins/).
